Audience

The Youth has to be aware of the public transit before being comfortable to use it.

One of the intended demographic groups that Transit Smart is aimed at is the young adult group (18 - 25 yrs).

There is an immense potential in this demographic, as this is the prime age for travelling — concerts, parties, musuems, restaurants, etc.

The language of the interface and system has been kept quirky to appeal to this group.

"I have no choice but to use the bus, I cannot afford a car. I have to deal with it at the end of the day."

It is a frustrating process, to get used to how the buses work, to navigate through the workings of it, transferring, bus schedules.

There is no Wi-fi access at home, and definitely no Wi-Fi access at bus stops.

Also, not all bus stops are located conveniently for everyone, it can be a 1 minute walk or a 15 minute one.

The inteface aims to overall contribute to the betterment of navigating through the bus system, but also act as a point of contact — new updates, bus stop closures, bus delays, it is all present there.
