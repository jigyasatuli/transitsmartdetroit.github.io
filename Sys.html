Transit Smart Detroit
1. Transit Smart Passenger Kiosk

A kiosk interface which simplifies the process of navigating through the bus systems. The various features are shown in the walkthrough videos below: — Bus Updates — Map system — Fare Purchase — Trip Planner — Emergency calling

2. Bus Wrap

The Bus Wrap contains a light- up map of route of the bus. This acts as an indicator to someone new to the bus system, the passersby. The light-up map helps in determining the journey completed by the bus. The branding is integrated as well, for recognition under the Transit Smart System.



3. Bus Stand + Kiosk Plans

Redesigning the bus stop and designing the kiosk was important to incorporate accessibility and inclusion.
The bus stops in and around Detroit do not have adequate shelter capability. This redesign proposes more seating, material consideration, and inclusive design. The same goes for the kiosk.





Accessibility Considerations

Kiosk:

— Color-blind sensitive:
The contrast in map colors to depict different lines helps in easier visual cognition.

— Voice Assistant/Sensor:
The voice assistant automatically activates within 5 seconds of inactivity by the kiosk user.


Bus Stop:

— Automatic Door Access: The buttons help in easier access of the door.

— Priority Seating: for people with disabilities.
