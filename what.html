Transit Smart Detroit

The bus systems running through Detroit are managed by different bus vendors, with a lack of connectivity between them.

As a result, there is less awareness and clarity on using the bus system.

Some common complaints/phrases about the buses:

"Which bus to use? Which route to take?"

"You must request a transfer from the driver and buy a separate transfer ticket."

"Where is the bus? Is it a reliable service? Is it safe?"

"I really want to use the bus but I am so confused..."

The Concept

An integration of the bus systems to provide easier access and a seamless travel experience, with a priority in increasing bus ridership.

Campaign: Transit Smart + Transit Smart Passenger

Transit Smart becomes this integrated and intelligent system of keeping the users and prospective users informed about the bus systems in Detroit. And the user becomes an informed, smart and alert passenger, i.e. a Transit Smart Passenger.
